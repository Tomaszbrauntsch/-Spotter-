About Spotter

20% of fatal road accidents involves the driver being exhausted
According to VicRoads Road Accident Facts Victoria, 1998 Edition, about 30% of severe single vehicle crashes in rural areas involve the driver being fatigued
This application prevents future crashes to occur, by tracking your face with other faces in our database and declaring if you are indeed tired or not
Usage: Simply left click, and it will tell you if you are good or should rest!
